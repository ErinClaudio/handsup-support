Privacy Policy

Hands Up - Youth Sports Team Communication App

Last Updated: September 24, 2025
Effective Date: September 24, 2025
COPPA Notice: This app is designed for youth sports teams and may collect information from children under 13. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting personal information from children.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: User role (parent, admin, coach)
  • Child Information: Names and team memberships (with parental consent)
  • Team Information: Team names, league associations, coach details

Communication Data

  • Messages: Team messages and communications (limited to 1000 characters)
  • Event Information: Schedules, game times, practice information
  • Notification History: Records of push notifications sent

Technical Information

  • Device Tokens: Push notification tokens for iOS devices
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, feature interactions
  • Platform Detection: Whether you're using iOS native app or web browser

2. How We Use Your Information

Primary Services

  • Facilitate team communication and messaging
  • Send push notifications for important updates and events
  • Manage team rosters, schedules, and events
  • Provide dashboard views for parents and coaches

Safety and Security

  • Authenticate user access and prevent unauthorized use
  • Protect against security threats and abuse
  • Maintain data integrity and system functionality

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information in these limited circumstances:

Within Your Team/League

  • Team messages are visible to all team members
  • Event schedules are shared with team participants
  • Coach and admin users can view team roster information

Service Providers

  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNs): For delivering push notifications
  • Hosting Services: For secure data storage and app functionality

Legal Requirements

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the safety of children or prevent harm
  • To enforce our terms of service

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Important: We take children's privacy seriously and comply with COPPA requirements.

Parental Consent

  • We obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting personal information from children under 13
  • Parents can review, delete, or refuse further collection of their child's information
  • Parents receive direct notice of our information practices

Limited Collection

  • We only collect information necessary for team communication and organization
  • No behavioral advertising or tracking for children
  • Child information is only shared within the team context with parental consent

Parental Rights

Parents have the right to:

  • Review personal information collected from their child
  • Request deletion of their child's personal information
  • Refuse to permit further collection or use of their child's information
  • Contact us with questions about our children's privacy practices

5. Push Notifications

Consent and Control

  • Push notifications require explicit user consent through iOS native prompts
  • You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings
  • We store device tokens securely for notification delivery

Notification Content

  • Team messages and announcements
  • Event reminders and schedule changes
  • Important team communications

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Encrypted password storage using industry-standard methods
  • Secure data transmission using HTTPS
  • Regular security assessments using tools like Brakeman
  • Access controls and user authentication
  • Protection against common security vulnerabilities

7. Data Retention

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active
  • Messages: Retained for team communication history
  • Child Information: Deleted when requested by parents or when account is closed
  • Device Tokens: Removed when app is uninstalled or notifications are disabled

8. Your Rights and Choices

Account Management

  • Update your personal information through the app
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Control notification preferences

Data Access

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)

9. International Users

Our services are hosted in the United States. If you are using our services from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email notifications to registered users
  • Updates to this webpage with a new "Last Updated" date

Email: privacy@limehummingbird.com
